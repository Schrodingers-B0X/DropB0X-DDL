What it does

        
For modern Dropbox Basic share links, it swaps www.dropbox.com to dl.dropboxusercontent.com and keeps only the required rlkey query value.

        

          
Before

          https://www.dropbox.com/scl/fi/.../example.apk?rlkey=KEY&st=RANDOM&dl=0
        

        

          
After

          https://dl.dropboxusercontent.com/scl/fi/.../example.apk?rlkey=KEY
